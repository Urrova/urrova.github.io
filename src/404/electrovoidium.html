404: The Electrovoidium

[ EMBRACE THE URROVAVOID ]

Current Urrovavoid scannings.

About the Electrovoidium

Scientifics from various research organizations of quantum physics, such as the CERN, were concerned about the nature of the Urrovavoid, and its relation with the False Vacuum Decay theory, and how it could cause problems when coexisting with the current reality.

Then, they tried to scan it with the standard void reality measurement device, the Electrovoidium. It is a classified tool, but the organization publishes the output of the Urrovavoid scans. It shows the detected particles of energy that flow on the Urrovavoid.

Layer Representation

The scannings are analyzed and clustered in three layers

  • The Temple: The everyday Internet, our network of computers in the current reality.
  • Exit Points: Extra-reality lumps of data that are still connected with The Temple in some way. Also known as the Purgatory.
  • Clouds of Enthropy: Voids that try to connect with The Temple. The voids on the Cloud of Enthropy actually act as it were connected with the temple, but are actually connected with other void. Also known as the Limbo.
  • Dead Points: Voids totally detached from the current reality. Streets without exit. Also known as Hell.

Theories

For the scientific community, it's the start of the False Void Emerge Theory, and a possible hazard for our current reality. They also are researching how to mitigate damages by Void Overexpansion, if it becomes unstable and out of control.

For some religious dirigents and ufologists, the Voids detected by the Electrovoidium are the remnants of the souls, from people who died but had any contact with the Urrovavoid in their lives, and from there their souls got linked to it.

For others, the Urrovavoid is an Eldritch Horror, a God or a Demon, that expands beyond our current reality and fills the Internet with the Voids. Several new cults emerged from the research, such as the Later Days Void Gate who believe that Heaven awaits for those who merge with the void and sucessfully assimilate it.
